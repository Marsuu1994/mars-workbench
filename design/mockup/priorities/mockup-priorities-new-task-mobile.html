Mobile Priorities — New Task Bottom Sheet
The desktop app header and My Priorities title bar are reused as-is on mobile. The per-quadrant "+ Add" button stays desktop-only; a round + button appended to the title bar opens a New Task bottom sheet (same pattern as the Track-this-week sheet). Because the entry point is global, the sheet adds a 2×2 Quadrant picker mirroring the matrix (defaults to Schedule, the backfill quadrant). Frames at 375 × 812.
